# Privacy Policy

**Crow's NestMQTT**
Effective Date: May 4, 2026

## Introduction

Crow's NestMQTT ("the Application") is committed to your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how information is handled when you use the Application.

## Data Collection

The Application does **not collect, transmit, or share** any personal data, usage analytics, or telemetry. No information is sent to the developer or any third party.

## Local Data Storage

The Application stores the following data **exclusively on your local device** in `%LocalAppData%\CrowsNestMqtt\`:

- **Application logs** — diagnostic logs for troubleshooting (automatically rotated)
- **Connection settings** — MQTT broker addresses, usernames, and preferences you configure
- **Exported messages** — saved only to file paths you explicitly choose

This data is never uploaded, synchronized, or shared. It remains entirely under your control and can be deleted at any time by removing the application data folder.

## Network Usage

The Application connects **only** to MQTT broker servers that you explicitly configure. These connections are used solely to subscribe to and publish MQTT messages as directed by you. No connections are made to any other servers, analytics endpoints, or third-party services.

## Third Parties

The Application does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy

Since the Application does not collect any data, it is compliant with applicable children's privacy regulations including COPPA.

## Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy is updated, the changes will be communicated through the Microsoft Store listing.
